Here are some cactus facts:

The Echinopsis genus is famous for their once-a-year, large flowers. Funnel shaped and beautiful, they sadly tend to last a day or two at most.

The Gymnocalycium Marsoneri species can grow in shade. It vigorously reproduces asexually when grown at home.

Opuntia, or Prickly Pear cacti, produce edible fruits. They are quite tasty, and a good source of magnesium.

Cactuses are related to beets. This is why they can produce Betalain: unique red pigments which most other plants don't have.
